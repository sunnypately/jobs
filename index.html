For the corridor, internal-medicine hospitalist base compensation clusters in the $270K–$310K band (Salary.com pegs VA ≈ $271K and MD ≈ $278K; Indeed/Marit report VA ≈ $296–310K). Nocturnist premiums, RVU bonuses, and shift differentials push top earners past $340K. Neonatology averages run a touch higher — Salary.com lists VA ≈ $291K and MD ≈ $298K, while survey-based sources (SalaryExpert, ERI) including bonus put the figure near $360K+, with academic/NICU-call roles and senior attendings reaching $400K+. Figures are market averages, not specific offers — always verify against MGMA benchmarks and the actual contract.
